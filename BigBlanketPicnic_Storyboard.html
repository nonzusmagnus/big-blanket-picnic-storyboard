BIG BLANKET PICNIC
IRELAND

Storyboard — Tech Script

Duration: ~38s  |  Format: 9:16 Vertical  |  1st August 3–9PM

CHEF

Lead. At the BBQ. Nigerian Pidgin English.

FRIEND

Lead. Stuck in traffic. Nigerian Pidgin English.

FEMALE FRIEND

Supporting. Irish accent.

BACKGROUND

2–3 people. Dancing, socialising.

BEAT 1

The Chef

00:00 – 00:05

Lens & Camera Angle

24mm, low angle dutch tilt slightly.

Equipment

Gimbal — slow push in from low as he speaks.

CHEF: "Oya, you dey come or not?"

BEAT 2

Stuck in Traffic

00:05 – 00:14

Lens & Camera Angle

24mm from the side, level with the window — wide enough to catch the surrounding traffic in frame while keeping him as the clear subject.

Equipment

Tripod — locked off at the side of the car, clean and still so the chaos of traffic around him does the work.

FRIEND: "Bros, I dey hold for traffic o, no vex."

BEAT 3

The Horn

00:14 – 00:19

Lens & Camera Angle

50mm from the side — tighter than Beat 2, pulling focus to his expression and reaction while traffic compresses behind him.

Equipment

Tripod — locked off, same side position as Beat 2.

FRIEND: "Abeg wait now, you no see we dey inside traffic!"

BEAT 4

No Blanket

00:19 – 00:24

Lens & Camera Angle

50mm from the side — tight on his face, window frame cutting the shot naturally, shallow depth of field.

Equipment

Tripod — locked off completely, same eyeline as Beats 2 and 3.

FRIEND: "Ah, see ehn — I no even get blanket o."

BEAT 5

The Save

00:24 – 00:28

Lens & Camera Angle

24mm — wide enough to hold both her and the Chef in frame with the event breathing behind them.

Equipment

Gimbal — slow drift follows her slightly as she exits back into the crowd.

FEMALE FRIEND (Irish accent): "Don't worry, we've got one here."

BEAT 6

The Sign-Off

00:28 – 00:34

Lens & Camera Angle

50mm side angle through the window on the Friend, 24mm on the Chef at the grill.

Equipment

Tripod on Friend, Gimbal on Chef — slow pull back revealing more of the BBQ setup.

FRIEND: "Okay okay, I dey come, I dey come."

CHEF: "Oya, make you hurry come — food don almost ready."

BEAT 7

Title Card

00:34 – 00:38

Lens & Camera Angle

8mm ground level for maximum field of view and dramatic scale, or drone wide if available.

Equipment

Tripod or drone — locked off.

MUSIC CARRIES — No dialogue
